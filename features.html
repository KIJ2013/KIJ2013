Features

This page is designed to list the main features of the app and describe the intended functionality.

News

Articles

Blog posts from KIJ website

Live

Twitter feed
Participants should be able to use familiar twitter features such as retweet, reply etc. after providing user details.

Events

Calendar of upcoming events
Reminder feature so participants don't miss events
Can be customised based subcamp preference.

Map

Large scale map of the site
Geolocation to pinpoint and help find location
Possibly highlight facilities as requested
Possibly show rudimentary directions
Possibly help those outside the site navigate to it.

Radio

The camp will be broadcasting a radio station
It should be available over the internet and thus streamable to devices
Consider including option to drop back to FM radio if device supports it.

Information

QR codes will be around the site for participants to scan in order to learn more on the topic.

Barcode

The app needs a facility to allow the scanning or at least interception of barcodes in the correct namespace.

Settings

Several things in the app will be configurable
Participants will be able to choose their subcamp for specific information.
